# Roadmap

### Launch phases:&#x20;

* **Q2-2024: Monetization for influencers and communities**
  * Soft launch period where users can invite max 3 friends (2 weeks period)
  * Airdrop farming period to earn points based on your social activity, to grow engagement (1.5 month period)
  * Token Generation Even with Launchpad
* **Q3-2024: Additional community integration**
  * Launch of additional monetization features
  * External community onboarding integrations
* **Q4-2024: AI integration with revenue sharing**
  * AI powered feeds
  * Launch of sector oriented communities\
